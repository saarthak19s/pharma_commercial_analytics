01 · The Problem

Reps visiting wrong doctors. Revenue leaking.

Core Problem

Pharma sales reps visit 800 doctors across 6 territories with no data-driven system to prioritize which doctors to visit, how often, or how to allocate their limited time. Result: high-value doctors under-visited, low-value doctors over-visited, revenue left on the table.

564 doctors
under-visited despite high revenue potential

Business Logic Chain

Patient visits doctor with illness
↓
Rep visits doctor → builds relationship
↓
Doctor prescribes our product (rx_count)
↓
rx_count × list_price = revenue
↓
Revenue → doctor tier → visit priority
↓
Rep attainment → incentive payout

03 · Key Findings

Three insights that drive decisions.

564

High-Value Doctors Under-Visited

564 doctors have significant prescription revenue potential but are not receiving recommended visit frequency. This is the single biggest revenue leak in the system.

272%

Rep_28 vs Team Average

Top rep achieved 272% attainment while team averages near 100%. Replicating Rep_28's doctor targeting strategy is the biggest performance lever available.

21×

OncoShield Price Premium

OncoShield at $950 generates 21× more revenue per prescription than RespiraClear at $45. Increasing oncologist visit frequency has highest revenue ROI per rep visit.

04 · Business Recommendations

$3–4M revenue opportunity identified.

01

Reallocate Rep Time to Under-Called Doctors

Shift visits from 120 over-called low-value doctors to 564 under-called high-value doctors. Tier 1 doctors averaging $33,700/year — each additional visit drives incremental prescription revenue.

~$1M
02

Replicate Rep_28 Strategy Across Bottom 10 Reps

Analyze Rep_28's territory, doctor selection, and product focus. Build a replicable playbook. If bottom 10 reps improve from 80% to 120% attainment — $200K improvement per rep × 10 reps.

~$2M
03

Prioritize OncoShield · Target Oncologists

Increase rep visit frequency to oncologists specifically. 10% increase in OncoShield prescriptions generates 21× more revenue than equivalent increase in RespiraClear.

~$1M
